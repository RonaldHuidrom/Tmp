Currently working on my thesis in low-dimensional topology particularly knot theory and braid groups, my interests in computer science and mathematics also include computational complexity theory and combinatorial game theory. I am a big fan of GNU/Linux and Emacs. I code in Python and C++. My GitHub account has some interesting repos including a solution manual I have written.

Besides mathematics, I am studying the violin and music theory, having received formal musical training from Bal Mukunda Music College. I read classics and Russian literature, especially Dostoevsky.
